Common Questions

When will BridgeHome be available?

Beta launch Q3 2026, public launch Q4 2026. We're building the most advanced family care platform.

Will my parent actually enjoy talking to AI?

Our AI is designed to feel like chatting with a caring friend, not a robot. 89% of elderly prefer voice interaction over apps, and our conversations focus on their interests, memories, and daily life in a completely natural way.

What if my parent says they're 'fine' when they're not?

BridgeHome helps families celebrate daily life together while noticing subtle changes. Our AI picks up on speech patterns, mood, and energy that families often miss in brief calls, helping you understand when 'fine' means truly flourishing.

How do I know when to be concerned vs. when to relax?

We believe technology should bring families closer, not create more anxiety. BridgeHome provides gentle, intelligent insights only when patterns suggest real opportunities to support your parent's wellbeing - helping you focus on connection rather than worry.

How much will it cost?

Pricing TBD based on user research. Goal: accessible to all families, likely similar to personal alarm services but with much more value.

Is this just surveillance?

No - we build connection, not surveillance. Elderly users control sharing, families get insights not monitoring data.

How is data protected?

Full GDPR compliance with medical-grade security. Data sharing only with explicit consent from elderly users.

How is this different from existing solutions?

First proactive, family-focused AI companion with advanced health analytics and seamless family connection features.
